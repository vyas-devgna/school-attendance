If something is wrong

  • App won't pair: ask the office admin to open this panel and make a new code, then scan/type it.
  • "Remote access is off": the office PC or its internet is down. Local Wi-Fi still works.
  • App shows old screens: close and reopen the installed app — it refreshes itself.
  • Device removed: the admin can restore it in the Devices tab.
